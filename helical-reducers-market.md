<p><p>The helical reducers market can be categorized into two main types: parallel-axis and perpendicular-axis helical gear reducers. Parallel-axis helical gear reducers feature gears that are aligned parallel to each other, providing efficient power transmission with minimal noise. They are commonly used in applications where space is limited. Perpendicular-axis helical gear reducers, on the other hand, have gears oriented at right angles, allowing for compact designs and versatile configurations. Both types are widely utilized in various industrial applications for their reliability and performance.</p></p>
