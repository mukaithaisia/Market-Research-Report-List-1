<p><ul><li>Off-site Regeration</li><li>On-site Regeeration</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The regenerated catalyst market comprises two main types: off-site and on-site regeneration. Off-site regeneration involves transporting used catalysts to specialized facilities for processing, ensuring efficient recovery and recycling. This method is often preferred for its ability to manage large volumes and complex catalyst types. In contrast, on-site regeneration occurs within the facility utilizing the catalyst, minimizing transportation costs and downtime. This method allows for immediate reuse, enhancing operational efficiency and reducing waste in catalytic processes.</p></p>
