<p><p>The polyolefin staple fiber market primarily includes two types: polyethylene staple fiber and polypropylene staple fiber. Polyethylene staple fiber is known for its excellent moisture resistance and durability, making it suitable for applications such as carpet backing and insulation materials. Polypropylene staple fiber, on the other hand, offers lightweight properties and high chemical resistance, which makes it ideal for use in construction textiles, automotive interiors, and filtration products. Both types are valued for their versatility and performance in various industries.</p></p>
