<p><p>The cytoskeleton market is delineated into three main types: actin filaments, intermediate filaments, and microtubules. Actin filaments are crucial for cell shape and movement, often utilized in muscle contraction and cell signaling. Intermediate filaments provide structural stability and resilience against mechanical stress, playing a vital role in maintaining cell integrity. Microtubules facilitate intracellular transport and are essential for cell division and organization. Together, these components support cellular functions, offering opportunities for research and therapeutic applications in various fields.</p></p>
