<p><p>Screw jacks are mechanical devices used to lift or move heavy loads through screw mechanisms. The market for screw jacks is primarily divided into two types: ball screw jacks and worm gear screw jacks. Ball screw jacks utilize ball bearings to reduce friction, allowing for higher efficiency and load capacity, making them suitable for precision applications. Worm gear screw jacks use a gear mechanism to achieve higher torque and provide reliable lifting, often employed in heavy-duty applications like construction and manufacturing.</p></p>
