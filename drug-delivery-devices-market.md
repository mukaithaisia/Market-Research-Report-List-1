<p><p>The drug delivery devices market consists of three main types: injection type, mucosal type, and implanted type. Injection type devices, such as syringes and auto-injectors, facilitate precise dosing through the bloodstream. Mucosal type devices, like nasal sprays and transdermal patches, target absorption through mucosal membranes for systemic effects. Implanted type devices, including pumps and drug-eluting implants, provide sustained medication release over extended periods, enhancing therapeutic effectiveness and patient compliance. Each type serves specific medical needs and application scenarios.</p></p>
