<p><ul><li>Single Level Ventilators</li><li>Bilevel Ventilators</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Non-invasive positive pressure ventilators for home use include two main types: single level ventilators and bilevel ventilators. Single level ventilators deliver a consistent pressure throughout the breathing cycle, making them suitable for patients with stable respiratory conditions. In contrast, bilevel ventilators provide two different pressure levels—one for inhalation and a lower one for exhalation—offering greater comfort and support for patients with fluctuating respiratory needs. Both types enhance breathing assistance while minimizing discomfort for users in home settings.</p></p>
