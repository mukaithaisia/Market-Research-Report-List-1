<p><p>Microwave Integrated Circuits (MIC) are essential components in high-frequency applications, with two main market types: Monolithic Microwave Integrated Circuits (MMIC) and Hybrid Microwave Integrated Circuits (HMIC). MMICs are fabricated entirely on a single semiconductor substrate, offering compactness and enhanced performance for applications like telecommunications and radar. In contrast, HMICs combine multiple discrete components on a single board, allowing for greater design flexibility and easier integration of various technologies. Both types are crucial for advancing microwave technology.</p></p>
