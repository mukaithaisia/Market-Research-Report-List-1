<p><p>The PCR technologies market encompasses various types, each catering to specific applications. Digital PCR offers precise quantification of nucleic acids. Real-Time Q-PCR enables simultaneous amplification and detection, providing immediate results. Standard PCR is the foundational method for DNA amplification. Assembly PCR facilitates the construction of complex DNA fragments. Inverse PCR is used for DNA mapping and mutation detection. Multiplex PCR allows simultaneous amplification of multiple targets, enhancing efficiency. Hot Start PCR minimizes non-specific reactions, improving specificity and yield in amplifications.</p></p>
