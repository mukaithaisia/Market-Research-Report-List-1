<p><strong>The Automotive Fasteners Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Threaded Fasteners</li><li>Non-threaded Fasteners</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive fasteners market is primarily divided into two types: threaded and non-threaded fasteners. Threaded fasteners, such as bolts and screws, are designed with helical ridges for secure assembly, allowing for easy disassembly and reassembly. Non-threaded fasteners, including rivets and pins, offer permanent joining solutions and are ideal for applications needing high shear strength. Both types are essential for ensuring structural integrity and safety in vehicles, catering to diverse manufacturing requirements across the automotive industry.</p></p>
