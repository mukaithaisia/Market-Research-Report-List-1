<p><p>The dryer cage market is categorized into two main types: heat-free and heated. Heat-free dryer cages utilize ambient air circulation to remove moisture from items without applying heat, making them ideal for delicate materials that may be damaged by high temperatures. In contrast, heated dryer cages use controlled heat to accelerate the drying process, effectively reducing drying time for bulkier items. Both types cater to different needs, balancing efficiency and material safety in various industries such as textiles and logistics.</p></p>
