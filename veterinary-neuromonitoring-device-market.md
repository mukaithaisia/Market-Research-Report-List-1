<p><p>The veterinary neuromonitoring device market is categorized into non-invasive and invasive types. Non-invasive devices, such as EEGs and transcranial magnetic stimulation, monitor neurological activity without penetrating the skin, offering comfort and safety for animals. Invasive devices, on the other hand, involve surgical procedures to implant electrodes or sensors directly into the nervous system for precise monitoring and diagnosis of neurological disorders. Both types are essential for diagnosing, monitoring, and managing neurological conditions in veterinary medicine.</p></p>
