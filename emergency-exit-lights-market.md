<p><ul><li>Maintained</li><li>Sustained(Non-maintained)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The emergency and exit lights market can be categorized into two main types: maintained and sustained (non-maintained) lighting. Maintained lights are designed to remain illuminated at all times, providing constant visibility. In contrast, sustained (non-maintained) lights are typically off during regular operation and only activate during emergencies or power outages. Both types serve crucial roles in ensuring safety during evacuations, with maintained lights providing continuous guidance, while sustained lights conserve energy until needed.</p></p>
