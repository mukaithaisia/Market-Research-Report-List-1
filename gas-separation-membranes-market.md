Hollow fiber membranes are designed with elongated, thin fibers that maximize surface area for efficient gas separation. Spiral wound membranes consist of flat sheets rolled into a spiral configuration, optimizing space and enhancing performance. Other types may include composite or asymmetric membranes, which combine various materials to achieve specific separation properties. Each type offers unique advantages tailored to different industrial applications.</p></p>
