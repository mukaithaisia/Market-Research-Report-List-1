<p><ul><li>Signal Intensity Registration</li><li>Pattern Recognition</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The EMG (electromyography) surface biosensors market is primarily categorized into two types: signal intensity registration and pattern recognition. Signal intensity registration focuses on measuring the strength of electrical signals generated by muscle contractions, providing quantitative data for applications like rehabilitation and prosthetics. Pattern recognition, on the other hand, involves analyzing these signals to identify specific muscle activity patterns, enabling applications in assistive devices and human-computer interfaces. Both types enhance the understanding and usage of muscular signals for medical and technological advancements.</p></p>
