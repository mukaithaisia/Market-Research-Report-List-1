<p><p>The felt-tip pen market comprises three main types: oily, water-based, and alcoholic felt-tip pens. Oily felt-tip pens feature opaque, fast-drying ink, making them suitable for glossy surfaces. Water-based felt-tip pens use non-toxic, water-soluble ink, ideal for easy blending and environmentally friendly applications, primarily in art and school settings. Alcoholic felt-tip pens contain ink that dries quickly and is resistant to water, often favored in professional art and graphic design due to their vibrant colors and reliability.</p></p>
