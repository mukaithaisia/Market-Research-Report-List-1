<p><p>Thermal Interface Materials (TIMs) for the 5G market are essential for efficient heat transfer between components. Thermal grease provides a liquid-like consistency for optimal filling of gaps, ensuring effective thermal conduction. Thermal gels offer similar properties but are often more viscous. Thermal pads are solid materials that offer ease of application and consistent performance. Phase Change Materials (PCMs) absorb and release heat as they change states, enhancing thermal management. Other TIMs may include adhesive films or specialized compounds tailored for specific applications, ensuring system reliability.</p></p>
