<p><p>The hops market consists of three primary types: bittering hops, aroma hops, and dual-purpose hops. Bittering hops are primarily used to impart bitterness to beer, balancing its sweetness. Aroma hops enhance the fragrance and flavor, contributing floral, fruity, or spicy notes essential for the sensory profile. Dual-purpose hops serve both functions, providing bitterness and aromatic qualities, making them versatile in brewing. Each type plays a critical role in the beer-making process, influencing the final product's taste and quality.</p></p>
