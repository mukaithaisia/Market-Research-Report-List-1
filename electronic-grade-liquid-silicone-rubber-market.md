<p><p>The Electronic Grade Liquid Silicone Rubber (LSR) market consists of two main types: Single Component LSR and Two Component LSR. Single Component LSR is ready to use and requires no mixing, providing convenience and uniformity for manufacturing. In contrast, Two Component LSR involves a separate curing agent that must be mixed before use, allowing for more flexibility in applications and properties. Both types are essential for producing high-performance electronic components, offering excellent thermal stability and electrical insulation.</p></p>
