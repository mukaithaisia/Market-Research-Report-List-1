<p><strong>The COVID-19 IgM/IgG Rapid Test Kits Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>RDT(Rapid Diagnostic Test)</li><li>ELISA(Enzyme-linked Immunosorbent Aassay)</li><li>Neutralization Assay</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The COVID-19 IgM/IgG rapid test kits market comprises three main types: Rapid Diagnostic Tests (RDT), Enzyme-linked Immunosorbent Assays (ELISA), and Neutralization Assays. RDTs provide quick, on-site results using a simple procedure, enabling immediate screening. ELISA tests are more sensitive and specific, requiring laboratory settings for accurate quantification of antibodies. Neutralization Assays are advanced tests that assess the functional ability of antibodies to inhibit viral entry, playing a crucial role in understanding immunity and vaccine efficacy.</p></p>
