<p><strong>The Aerospace Optical Fiber Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single Mode</li><li>Muti Mode</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The aerospace optical fiber market primarily consists of two types: single-mode and multi-mode fibers. Single-mode fibers have a small core diameter, allowing only one light mode to propagate, which enables high-speed data transmission over long distances with minimal signal loss. In contrast, multi-mode fibers have a larger core, supporting multiple light modes, making them suitable for shorter distances and higher bandwidth applications within aerospace systems. Both types are essential for improving communication, navigation, and control in modern aerospace technologies.</p></p>
