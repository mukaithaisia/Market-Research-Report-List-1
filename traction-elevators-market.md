<p><strong>The Traction Elevators Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Geared Traction Elevators</li><li>Gearless Traction Elevators</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The traction elevators market is primarily divided into two types: geared and gearless traction elevators. Geared traction elevators utilize a motor with gears to lift the elevator cab, making them suitable for mid-rise buildings due to their efficiency and cost-effectiveness. In contrast, gearless traction elevators use a direct drive system, providing smoother and quieter operation, ideal for high-rise buildings. Both types are energy-efficient, offering advanced control systems that enhance performance and passenger comfort in vertical transportation.</p></p>
