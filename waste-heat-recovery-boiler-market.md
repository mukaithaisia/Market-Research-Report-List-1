<p><p>The Waste Heat Recovery Boiler market comprises primarily three types: Water Tube Boilers, Fire Tube Boilers, and Others. Water Tube Boilers utilize water-filled tubes to absorb heat from combustion gases, making them efficient for high-pressure applications. Fire Tube Boilers, conversely, have hot gases passing through tubes surrounded by water, suitable for lower pressure and simpler configurations. The "Others" category includes specialized designs and hybrid systems, catering to specific industrial requirements and enhancing energy efficiency across various sectors.</p></p>
