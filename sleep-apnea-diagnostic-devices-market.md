<p><p>The sleep apnea diagnostic devices market is primarily categorized into screening-type and stereotype devices. Screening-type devices, such as home sleep apnea tests (HSAT), are designed for initial assessment and are user-friendly, allowing patients to monitor sleep patterns in their home environment. Stereotype devices refer to more complex, often laboratory-based polysomnography systems that provide comprehensive data on sleep stages, oxygen levels, and heart rate, offering detailed insights into sleep disorders for accurate diagnosis and treatment planning.</p></p>
