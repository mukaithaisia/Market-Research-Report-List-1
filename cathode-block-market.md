<p><p>The cathode block market consists of three primary types: semi-graphitic, graphitic, and graphitized. Semi-graphitic blocks contain a mix of graphite and other materials, offering moderate conductivity and thermal resistance. Graphitic blocks are composed primarily of graphite, providing high conductivity and thermal properties, making them suitable for demanding applications. Graphitized blocks, produced through high-temperature treatment, achieve superior conductivity and structural integrity, making them ideal for high-performance uses in aluminum smelting and other industrial processes.</p></p>
