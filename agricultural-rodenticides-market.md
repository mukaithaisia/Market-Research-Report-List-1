<p><strong>The Agricultural Rodenticides Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>"Anticoagulants Rodenticides"</li><li>"Non-anticoagulants Rodenticides"</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The agricultural rodenticides market is primarily divided into two types: anticoagulants and non-anticoagulants. Anticoagulant rodenticides inhibit blood clotting, leading to fatal hemorrhage in rodents, and are popular for their effectiveness over multiple feedings. Non-anticoagulant rodenticides, on the other hand, act through different mechanisms, such as disrupting the nervous system or causing dehydration, often requiring one-time consumption for efficacy. Both types are essential for managing rodent populations in agriculture, ensuring crop protection and minimizing economic losses.</p></p>
