<p><p>The mobile disinfection cabinet market features various disinfection methods tailored for efficient sterilization. High-pressure steam disinfection utilizes heat and pressure to eliminate pathogens swiftly, ideal for heat-resistant items. Electrothermal disinfection employs electric heating elements to achieve high temperatures for effective sanitization. Ozone disinfection utilizes ozone gas, known for its high oxidative properties, to break down contaminants. Ultraviolet disinfection leverages UV light to disrupt the DNA of microorganisms, rendering them incapable of reproduction, making it suitable for rapid surface and air sterilization.</p></p>
