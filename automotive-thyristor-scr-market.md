<p><strong>The Automotive Thyristor SCR Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Unidirectional Thyristor</li><li>Bidirectional Thyristor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive thyristor SCR market can be divided into two main types: unidirectional and bidirectional thyristors. Unidirectional thyristors allow current to flow in one direction, making them suitable for applications like automotive lighting and motor control. In contrast, bidirectional thyristors can conduct current in both directions, which makes them ideal for applications such as AC motor control and power conversion systems. Both types play crucial roles in enhancing the efficiency and reliability of automotive electronics.</p></p>
