<p><strong>The Solid-State Gas Sensors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Galvanic Oxygen Sensor</li><li>Catalytic Bead Sensor</li><li>Semiconducting Oxide Sensor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Solid-state gas sensors encompass various technologies for detecting and measuring gas concentrations. The galvanic oxygen sensor operates through an electrochemical reaction that generates a voltage proportional to oxygen levels. Catalytic bead sensors detect combustible gases by measuring changes in temperature caused by combustion on a heated bead. Semiconducting oxide sensors respond to gas concentrations through changes in electrical resistance, often utilized for detecting a variety of gases by altering their conductive properties in response to target gases.</p></p>
