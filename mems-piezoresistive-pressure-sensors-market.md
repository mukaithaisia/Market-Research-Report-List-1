<p><strong>The MEMS Piezoresistive Pressure Sensors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Absolute Pressure Sensors</li><li>Relative (Gauge) Pressure Sensors</li><li>Differential Pressure Sensors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>MEMS piezoresistive pressure sensors are classified into three main types based on measurement conditions. Absolute pressure sensors measure pressure relative to a perfect vacuum, providing accurate readings across various applications. Relative (gauge) pressure sensors measure pressure against the ambient atmospheric pressure, often used in automotive and HVAC systems. Differential pressure sensors assess the difference between two pressures, making them ideal for flow measurements and process control. Each type serves distinct applications, driving advancements in various industries.</p></p>
